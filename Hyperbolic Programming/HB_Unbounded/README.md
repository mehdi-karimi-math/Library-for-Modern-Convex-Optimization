This folder contains a file for creating instances involving two hyperbolicty cones. The formulation is

$$
\begin{array}{rrl}
\min  & \sum_{i=1}^n  ({x} \pm {y})_i   &   \\
\text{s.t.}  &   {x}   & \in \Lambda_+(p_1,{e_1})    \\
  &   {y}   & \in \Lambda_+(p_2,{e_2})  
\end{array}
$$

The problem is unbounded when the objective is $\sum_{i=1}^n  ({x} - {y})_i$.
